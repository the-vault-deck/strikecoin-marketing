THE STRIKE COIN

APPEND-ONLY REALITY TESTING

A diagnostic instrument to determine if a small business is structurally viable for the operator.

Entrepreneurship is a tool, not a moral imperative.
This system reveals viability through structure, not persuasion.

CAPABILITY & CONSTRAINT MAPPER

Inputs: available hours, obligations, physical capacity, risk tolerance, transportation/tools access, financial runway.

Output: structural path compatibility.

MINIMUM VIABLE OFFER TEST

Defines one offer, one customer type, one price, one outreach method.

All attempts logged append-only.

REALITY FEEDBACK LOOP

After 10 outreach attempts: pattern exposure only.

No interpretation. Signal detection only.

REVENUE & EFFORT LEDGER

Tracks revenue, hours, expenses.

Displays effective hourly rate.

EXIT GATE PROTOCOL

Terminal review after fixed period.

Outputs: continue, refine, pause, or return to employment.

INACTION IS RECORDED

The system does not prompt action.
It records behavior.

If zero outreach occurs over time,
that data point remains visible.

No correction. No pressure.
